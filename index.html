The daily picture: At a blended ~26% conversion rate (digital + in-person), 15 leads = ~4 enrollments. Digital alone runs at 20% — that's your floor. 30 leads/day is the campaign target. Every table event lifts the blended rate because in-person converts at 35–40%, so the more in-person conversations you have, the fewer total leads you need.

System marks the lead responsive and sends a notification. Community Manager takes over manually in Studio Inbox from here.

Conversation stalls → autodraft assists
If the CM replies but the lead goes quiet, Studio Inbox creates an autodraft follow-up after 48 hours — up to 3 times. After that, the lead is marked inactive.
